How important of having online portfolio?

An online portfolio is a crucial tool in today's professional world. It's like a personalized showcase of your skills and accomplishments. Firstly, it acts as a visual resume, displaying your work in a dynamic way. Unlike a paper resume, it can include images, videos, and examples of your projects. Secondly, it helps you express your personality and style. You can design it to reflect who you are and what makes you unique. Another key aspect is its flexibility. You can update it easily, showing your growth and adaptability in your field. Moreover, it's a great way to connect with potential employers or collaborators. You can share it easily online, making it a conversation starter. Lastly, it often sets you apart from others. It gives concrete evidence of your skills and achievements, which can be a deciding factor in opportunities. In short, having an online portfolio is essential. It's a versatile tool that showcases your abilities, personality, and growth, makingg it crucial in today's professional landscape.
